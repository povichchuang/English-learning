### Perfectionist (n.) (2026-04-21)

**Meaning**: 完美主義者 (a person who refuses to accept any standard short of perfection)

**Synonyms**: meticulous person, stickler, perfectionist

**Antonyms**: (none commonly used)

**Example**:
- He is a true perfectionist when it comes to his work.
- Her perfectionism can sometimes slow down her progress.
- As a perfectionist, she spent hours adjusting the final details.
- The designer's perfectionist approach ensured a flawless product.

### Anecdote (n.) (2026-04-21)

**Meaning**: 軼事 / 趣聞 (a short amusing or interesting story about a real incident or person)

**Synonyms**: story, tale, narrative, reminiscence

**Antonyms**: (none commonly used)

**Example**:
- He shared a funny anecdote about his childhood.
- The book is filled with interesting anecdotes about the scientist.
- She told a brief anecdote to break the ice.
- His speech included several touching anecdotes.
