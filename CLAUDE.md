## How I Work

This is NOT a coding project. You should help me:

1. **Add phrases** - When I give you a phrase, write it to `phrases.md` with examples
2. **Add vocabulary** - When I give you a word, write it to `vocabulary.md` with meaning and examples

- (prep.) - preposition 介詞
- (conj.) - conjunction 連詞
- (pron.) - pronoun 代詞
- (pv.) - phrasal verb 片語動詞
