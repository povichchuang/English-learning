# 101 句公式 (101 Sentence Formulas)

> Source: Extracted from 101句公式.pdf (a blank template PDF - content likely from user's notes)

## 1. Expressions of Gratitude & Support

### Formula 1: [Noun] + made my day

**Example**: You really made my day.

**Variations**:
- You really made my day.
- That made my day.

**Compliments**: You are considerate, diligent and genuine.

**Reaction**: No one has ever said this to me before.

---

### Formula 2: Thank you for + [Noun / V-ing]

**Examples**:
- Thank you for accompanying me.
- Thank you for supporting me.
- Thank you for your help.

---

### Support

**Willingness to Help**:
- I'll always have your back.
- I'm happy to help.
- I'll be happy to hear you out.

---

## 2. Responses & Small Talk

### General Thanks

- Thanks anyway (還是謝謝你)
- I owe you one.
- What would I do without you.
- You shouldn't have (你太客氣了)

### Responses to "Thank You"

- Anytime / Sure.
- Don't mention it.
- It was nothing / No worries.
- The pleasure is mine.
- Thanks for giving me a heads-up.

---

## 3. Satisfaction, Relief, and Frustration

### Satisfaction

- I'm happy with [something]
- Satisfied with [something]
- Pleased with [something]

### Relief

- It's a relief to [Verb].
- I'm so relieved to [Verb].
- I'm relieved at [Noun].

### Checking In

- Are you okay?
- Are you all right?
- Is everything ok?

### Responses to "How are you?"

- Never (been) better.
- I'm good / fine / okay.
- Not really / Not so good / Could be better.
- I've been better (不太好)

### Annoyance & Anger

- I'm tired of [V-ing].
- I'm mad at [someone].
- I'm sick of [Noun / V-ing].
- I can't stand [V-ing].
- I'm fed up with [V-ing].
- I've had enough of [Noun].

### Confrontation

- What's wrong? / What's going on?
- What's the problem/matter (with you)?
- I'm done (我受夠了)
- I'm so done with you (我受夠你了)
- That's it (真的夠了)
- Cut it out (好了喔)

---

## 4. Worry, Encouragement, and Disbelief

### Worry

- I'm worried about [Noun / V-ing].
- I'm concerned about [something].
- Don't worry about it.
- There's nothing to worry about.

### Anxiety

- Why are you so on edge / edgy? (你為何這麼焦慮)

### Optimism

- Things will get better!
- Things are looking up!

### Encouragement

- Pep talk (鼓勵)
- Hang in there.

### Disbelief & Criticism

- I'm upset with [someone].
- I'm disappointed with [something].
- I can't believe you [Past Tense Verb].
- This can't be true / real / right.
- This can't be happening.
- You are way out of line.

---

## 5. Asking for Help & Clarification

### Asking for Help

- Could you [Verb]?
- Could I ask you to [Verb]?
- Would you mind [V-ing]?
- I'd appreciate it if you could [Verb].
- Could you do me a favor?

### Clarification

- What do you mean by [something]?
- Could you explain what you mean?
- I'm not sure I understand.
- Could you say that again?
- Sorry, could you repeat that?

---

## 6. Making Plans & Suggestions

### Making Plans

- Let's [Verb].
- How about [V-ing]?
- What about [V-ing]?
- Why don't we [Verb]?
- We should [Verb].
- We ought to [Verb].

### Suggestions

- I suggest [V-ing].
- Have you thought about [V-ing]?
- Maybe we could [Verb].
- It might be better to [Verb].
- How about we [Verb]?

---

## 7. Agreeing & Disagreeing

### Agreeing

- Absolutely!
- I agree completely.
- That's exactly what I think.
- You're right.
- I couldn't agree more.

### Disagreeing

- I see your point, but...
- I'm not sure I agree.
- I don't think that's right.
- That's not how I see it.
- I have to disagree.

---

## 8. Giving Opinions

### Expressing Opinions

- In my opinion...
- I think that...
- I believe that...
- From my point of view...
- As I see it...

### Asking for Opinions

- What's your opinion?
- What do you think about...?
- How do you feel about...?
- Do you have any thoughts on...?

---

## 9. Apologizing & Sympathy

### Apologizing

- I'm sorry.
- I apologize for [V-ing].
- I'm really sorry about that.
- Please accept my apologies.
- That's my fault.

### Sympathy

- I'm sorry to hear that.
- That's too bad.
- I feel for you.
- What a shame!
- That's unfortunate.

---

## 10. Requests & Permissions

### Requests

- Could I have...?
- May I [Verb]?
- Would you mind if I [Verb]?
- Do you mind if I [Verb]?
- Is it okay if I [Verb]?

### Permissions

- Go ahead.
- Feel free.
- You can [Verb].
- It's fine.
- No problem.
